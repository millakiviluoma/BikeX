BikeX

Bikes Ltd is launching a new innovative product BikeX.

The development and design of BikeX started in 2013, and now it is fully functioning and ready to take over the world.

Design

We have designed a brand new bike concept without any unnecessary clutter taking the spotlight away from the beautiful, minimalistic design. You will be the talk of the town with BikeX.

The matte coloring and the geometric shape of the bike makes others admire your sense of style.



Features

BikeX has up to 9 gears, an ultra-light body and innovative wheel technology, the bike weighs just 12 pounds and it can turn from a cool city bike into design transportation for longer trips with just a little twist.

Technical details
Weight 12 lb
Gears 3 / 5 / 9
Price 359 €
Framesize 20 in
Standover height 21 in
Wheel size 22 in

Customization

The bike comes in 3 different colors: black, white, and grey, all painted with matte paint.

You can also tune the numbers of gears for our bike: 3, 5 or 9. There are two different models at the moment, one for the ladies and one for the sirs.


Availability
